Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat (usually beef, sometimes chicken). Pho is a popular food in Vietnam where it is served in households, street stalls and restaurants countrywide. Residents of the city of Nam Dinh were the first to create Vietnamese traditional pho. Pho is considered Vietnam's national dish.
